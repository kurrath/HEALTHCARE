Eye Strain: Causes, Symptoms & How to Reduce It 👀💡

1. Causes of Eye Strain

  • 🔹 Too much screen time – Staring at phones, computers, or TVs for long hours.
  • 🔹 Poor lighting – Too bright or too dim lighting strains the eyes.
  • 🔹 Not blinking enough – Eyes get dry when focusing too long.
  • 🔹 Wrong screen distance – Holding a phone too close or sitting too far from a computer.

2. Symptoms of Eye Strain

  • 👁 Tired, dry, or burning eyes
  • 👁 Blurred vision or difficulty focusing
  • 👁 Headaches or dizziness
  • 👁 Neck & shoulder pain (from poor posture)
  • 👁 Increased light sensitivity

3. How to Reduce & Prevent Eye Strain

✅ Follow the 20-20-20 Rule

📌 Every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds. This relaxes eye muscles.

✅ Adjust Your Screen & Lighting

  • 💻 Use Blue Light Filters – Turn on night mode on your phone/laptop.
  • 💡 Adjust Brightness – Match screen brightness with your surroundings.
  • 🛑 Reduce Glare – Use an anti-glare screen or avoid direct light on your screen.

✅ Blink More & Keep Eyes Moist

  • 👀 Blink often to prevent dryness.
  • 💦 Use eye drops if your eyes feel dry.

✅ Improve Posture & Distance

  • 🪑 Keep your computer screen at eye level and an arm’s length away.
  • 📱 Don’t hold your phone too close to your face.

✅ Eye Exercises

  • 1️⃣ Palming – Rub hands together and gently place them over closed eyes for relaxation.
  • 2️⃣ Eye Rolling – Slowly roll your eyes in circles to relieve tension.
  • 3️⃣ Focus Shifting – Look at a near object, then a far one, back and forth.

✅ Rest & Sleep Well

  • 😴 Get 7-8 hours of sleep to allow your eyes to recover.
  • ❌ Avoid screens 30-60 minutes before bed to reduce strain.

4. Foods for Healthy Eyes 🥦🥕

  • 🥕 Carrots – Rich in vitamin A for better vision.
  • 🥦 Green leafy veggies – Prevents eye damage.
  • 🐟 Fish (Salmon, Tuna) – High in Omega-3 for eye health.
  • 🥜 Nuts & Seeds – Protects against dry eyes.
