⏳ How to Overcome Time Management Issues in Academics

📝 Create a Study Plan
  • List all tasks, assignments, and exams with deadlines.
  • Use a planner or digital tools to track your schedule.
🎯 Set Priorities
  • Identify the most urgent and important tasks first.
  • Break large tasks into smaller, manageable steps.
⏰ Avoid Procrastination
  • Start tasks as soon as possible instead of delaying.
  • Use the Pomodoro technique to stay focused.
📅 Use a Time-Blocking Method
  • Allocate specific time slots for studying and breaks.
  • Stick to your schedule to build consistency.
🚀 Eliminate Distractions
  • Keep your phone away or use apps to block distractions.
  • Choose a quiet place for studying.
🌟 Stay Consistent
  • Develop a daily routine and stick to it.
  • Make small improvements each day to build better habits.
🤝 Seek Help When Needed
  • Ask teachers or classmates for guidance if struggling.
  • Join study groups to stay accountable.
