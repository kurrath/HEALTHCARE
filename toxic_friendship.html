🚫 How to Overcome a Toxic Friendship

🔍 Identify Toxic Traits
  • Constant criticism or belittling
  • Manipulation or guilt-tripping
  • Controlling behavior
  • Betrayal or gossiping behind your back
  • Draining your energy instead of supporting you
🚧 Set Boundaries
  • Reduce how much personal information you share.
  • Limit the time you spend together.
  • Stand up for yourself when they cross the line.
🏃‍♂️ Slowly Distance Yourself
  • Spend more time with positive and supportive friends.
  • Get involved in new activities or clubs.
  • Be polite but firm in keeping a healthy distance.
🗣️ Communicate If Necessary
  • Use “I” statements: “I feel hurt when you do X.”
  • Stay calm and express your need for space.
🤝 Seek Support
  • Talk to a trusted teacher, counselor, or family member.
  • Surround yourself with people who uplift and support you.
💪 Focus on Self-Growth
  • Engage in hobbies, sports, or things that make you happy.
  • Practice self-care and build confidence.
✅ Accept and Move On
  • It’s okay to let go of a friendship that no longer serves you.
  • Real friends bring out the best in you, not the worst.
