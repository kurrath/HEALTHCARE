🛁 Bathing & Body Hygiene

  • ✅ Take a bath daily to remove sweat, dirt, and bacteria. Use mild soap and focus on areas like underarms, feet, and private parts.
  • ✅ Wash your face at least twice a day to remove oil, dirt, and pollution.
  • ✅ Brush twice a day (morning & night) and floss daily to prevent cavities and bad breath.
  • ✅ Wash hands with soap before eating, after using the restroom, and after touching dirty surfaces.
  • ✅ Keep feet clean and dry to avoid infections or bad odor.

💇 Hair & Scalp Hygiene

  • ✅ Wash hair 2-3 times a week (more if oily).
  • ✅ Use a mild shampoo and massage the scalp properly to remove dirt and oil.
  • ✅ Avoid sharing combs to prevent dandruff or lice.
  • ✅ Trim hair regularly to maintain a neat appearance.

💅 Nail Hygiene

  • ✅ Trim nails once a week to avoid dirt buildup.
  • ✅ Avoid biting nails (it spreads germs).
  • ✅ Clean under nails when washing hands.

👕 Clothing & Laundry Hygiene

  • ✅ Wear clean clothes daily, especially underwear and socks.
  • ✅ Change clothes after sweating to prevent bacteria buildup.
  • ✅ Wash bed sheets and pillow covers weekly to avoid skin problems.

🩸 Menstrual Hygiene

  • ✅ Change sanitary pads/tampons every 4-6 hours to prevent infections.
  • ✅ Wash the genital area with warm water and mild soap.
  • ✅ Dispose of menstrual products properly.
