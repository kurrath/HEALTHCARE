🚨 Overcoming Sexual Abuse Issues in Academics

🗣️ Speak Up and Seek Help
  • Report the abuse to a trusted teacher, counselor, or guardian.
  • Contact helplines or organizations specializing in abuse support.
🤝 Surround Yourself with Support
  • Talk to friends or family members who can offer emotional support.
  • Join survivor support groups for guidance and healing.
💡 Know Your Rights
  • Understand that no one has the right to abuse you.
  • Learn about school policies and legal actions that protect you.
🧘‍♀️ Take Care of Your Mental Health
  • Engage in therapy or counseling for emotional recovery.
  • Practice mindfulness, journaling, or meditation to cope with stress.
🚪 Create Safe Boundaries
  • Avoid situations or individuals that make you uncomfortable.
  • Speak up against inappropriate behavior and seek intervention.
🎓 Focus on Your Education
  • Set academic goals to regain confidence and control over your future.
  • Use school resources to support your learning and well-being.
